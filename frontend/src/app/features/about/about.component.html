About kotha

What is Kotha?

Kotha is a modern rental platform where people can effortlessly discover properties or list their own — all in one place. Whether you're searching for a cozy studio or leasing out a multi-unit building, Kotha makes the process seamless, secure, and smart.

Who We Are

We're a team of passionate developers, designers, and problem-solvers dedicated to transforming the way people discover and manage rental properties. Whether you're a tenant searching for your next home or a landlord managing multiple listings, our platform is designed to make the experience seamless, secure, and smart.
